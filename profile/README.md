## Medicann

Medicann is an award-winning medical cannabis clinic providing expert, patient-centric care across the UK, Jersey, Guernsey and the Isle of Man.

We help eligible patients access safe, regulated treatment through fully virtual consultations, specialist-led prescribing, and secure home delivery.

### Our focus
- Personalised care from experienced clinicians
- Accessible, transparent pathways for eligible patients
- Responsible prescribing within current UK regulation
- Ongoing support that puts patient wellbeing first
